Data

The Jupyter notebook `(view here) <https://nbviewer.jupyter.org/github/allixender/30MapChallenge2020/blob/main/20/day-20.ipynb>`_ is provided in my `GitHub repo <https://github.com/allixender/30MapChallenge2020/tree/main/20>`_.
