Data
~~~~

Proportion of population 60 and older in Estonian local governments as of 2019 and by COVID-19 infected counties


Population data: Estonian Statistics Board (https://www.stat.ee/)

COVID-19 data:

- Estonian Health Board Koroonakaart (https://www.terviseamet.ee/et/koroonaviirus/koroonakaart)
- Data on the current situation of the coronavirus (Estonian Open Data Portal) (https://opendata.riik.ee/andmehulgad/)

Base map: Leaflet, OpenStreetMap contributors, CartoDB


The Jupyter notebook `(view here) <https://nbviewer.jupyter.org/github/allixender/30MapChallenge2020/blob/main/25/day-25.ipynb>`_ is provided in my `GitHub repo <https://github.com/allixender/30MapChallenge2020/tree/main/25>`_.
